About Us

Founded in 2020 by Shubhangi Bhandari & Sanya Gupta who are Engineers by Profession but Baker by passion . They started Cake Villa Because they wanted to follow their passion Cake Villa has created and maintained products baked with the finest ingredients that are as pleasing to the eye as they are to the palate. the Cake Villa has impressed thousands of customers with its artistic tortes, cakes, and pastries that really do taste better than they look. We the people of Cake Villa are extraordinarily proud to represent the fantastic products of Cake Villa Bakery . By maintaining the highest standards for quality and freshness of ingredients, mixing the unique and proven recipes to the most exacting standards, and artistically decorating our products, we are certain to provide a consistent bakery product to our customers every time. With that knowledge in mind, we will never disappoint them by allowing our quality control and high standards to diminish. We are confident that with your first order, and your first bite, that you will become a new loyal customer. We know that you will be as proud as we are to offer these outstanding desserts to your guests. We hope that you will give us a try. We know you will be glad that you did.
